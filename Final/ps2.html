Playstation 2

About

The Playstation 2 was the best selling home console
in the world, selling over 155 million units. With other
companies like Nintendo and Xbox trying to come into the
competition Playstation had to come out with something good,
and they did.

Early stages

The Playstation 2 was released in 2000, 6 years after the first console release. The console was positioned as
a competitor to the Sega dreamcast, the first of the sixth-generation consoles. On the first day of release Sony
raked in $250 million beating the previous record by over $100 million. Directly after the console was released,
it was difficult to find the consoles on the shelves at stores so it was sold on ebay for thousands of dollars. At that time
Sega went under a lot of fire and discontinued their console leaving the ps2 alone for a couple of years.
