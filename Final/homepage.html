Playstation

Background Information

Playstation is a company made from sony that makes video game consoles and video
game accessories. The company was made in 1994 and released their first console in 1998.
Since then, they have released 3 other consoles and have also gone into handheld devices.
The main reason Playstation has been so succesful is from Playstation network, their own
social gaming service where you could play with other people online via the internet. Playstation is the
leading company in the gaming console industry and now are about to create many shock waves
with their VR(virtual reality) device.

History

Playstation was created from a failed partnership with Nintendo that infuriated the head of Sony
and in turn Playstation was created as a rival to Nintendo and their line of consoles. Playstation released
their first console in 1998 to America but it was released in 1994 in Japan. They followed this up with the PS1
and the PS2 which released fairly closely to eachother in the 2000's. The Playstation 2 created a huge spark
as it was the fastest console ever to sell 100,000,000 units. After this Playstation went onto release the Playstation 3
and the Playstation 4. The PS3 was very succesful selling over 100,000,000 units and the PS4 was the fastest console
ever to sell 1,000,000 units, this was in less than 24 hours of the console releasing. Playstation has released many
variations of the consoles but these are the main consoles.

Playstation network

Playstation network is a entertaintment service that was first introduced in the Playstation 3 and has now expanded to much
more. Playstation network was created for the PS3 but has endeavored into many other area's like smartphones and tablets. Playstation
network also has services that people could use to their advantage. Some services include, Playstation Plus, Playstation Music and Playstation
Video. The PS2 had some games that you could play online against other people but it wasn't integrated into the system. Then with the release of
their new console they decided to integrate it and they named it The Playstation network. The Playstation Network gave people online ID's that they
are known as in the Playstation world, with this they can collect trophies from games, add other friends and level up in games.
