Playstation 3

About

The Playstation 3 is the 3rd console out of the Playstation
lineup. It was released in 2006 but this around
Playstation had some competition since microsoft had
released their own console called the Xbox, this console
goes on to be Sony's main competitor in the future.

Early stages

The Playstation 3 was released in 2006 around November all around the world. Reports showed that there
was a lot of violence surrounding the launch of the Ps3, many people got robbed for their consoles. The console
was delayed in many places around the world at first, this lead to many people firing shots at Sony but then
Sony told the people they could get a refund or wait. It was a shakey start but the Ps3 still went on to be very
successful.
